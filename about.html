About Us

The Asiatic Society is one of India's oldest and most esteemed institutions dedicated to the promotion of knowledge and culture. Our collections span various fields, including archaeology, history, and the arts, showcasing the rich heritage of the subcontinent.

We invite you to explore our museum, participate in our programs, and engage with our digital archives to discover the depth of India's cultural history.

Join us in our mission to preserve, document, and celebrate the diversity of Indian culture and history.
