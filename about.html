About Us

The Asiatic Society is one of India's oldest and most esteemed institutions dedicated to the promotion of knowledge and culture. Our collections span various fields, including archaeology, history, and the arts, showcasing the rich heritage of the subcontinent.The Asiatic Society publishes books in different series, viz., Bibliotheca Indica, Monograph, Seminar & Public Lecture, Catalogues & Bibliographical Works besides some books under Miscellaneous publications. Moreover, it publishes periodicals, i.e. a quarterly Journal of the Asiatic Society and Monthly Bulletin of the Asiatic Society and some booklets on different occasions etc.“The Bound of investigations will be the geographic limits of Asia, and within these limits its enquiries will be extended to whatever is performed by man or produced by nature” – a statement contained in the memorandum of Articles of the Asiatic Society was prepared by Sir William Jones in that historic meeting. Thus began the long journey of the Asiatic Society, Kolkata.

We invite you to explore our museum, participate in our programs, and engage with our digital archives to discover the depth of India's cultural history.

Join us in our mission to preserve, document, and celebrate the diversity of Indian culture and history.
